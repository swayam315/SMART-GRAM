About SMART GRAM

SMART GRAM is an innovative platform developed under the PM-AJAY scheme to transform SC-majority villages into Model Villages (Adarsh Grams). Our mission is to bridge the infrastructure gap through technology, community participation, and data-driven governance.

We empower villagers to report their needs, enable government officials to make informed decisions, and ensure transparent execution of development projects.
